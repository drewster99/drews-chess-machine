Raise entropy_bonus from 0.001 to 0.01 (10x). The baseline trajectory shows the network monotonically concentrating probability mass onto a single illegal move, with entropy collapsing fast enough that arena promotion of a collapsed candidate locks in the failure. With entropy_bonus=0.001, the -beta*H(p) term in the loss is about two orders of magnitude too weak to resist the policy-gradient pressure toward one-hot logits on raw (unmasked) outputs — illegal_mass rose from 0.80 to 1.00 in minutes. Bumping to 0.01 keeps the counter-pressure on the same order as a typical per-sample policy CE contribution, which should hold entropy high enough that legal moves can continue to be distinguished and illegal_mass can keep falling, without being so large that it prevents any sharpening at all (0.01 is a common lc0/AlphaZero-style entropy-reg magnitude). This is a single-concept change and will produce observable signal within the 10-minute harness window via the illegal_mass_sum and max probes.
